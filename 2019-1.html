Week 1: The season begins!

Our team begins the trek down to the ice! First stop is Christchurch, New Zealand to gear up.

After 24 hours of travel from Calirfornia to Christchurch, our first stop is the Clothing Distribution Center (CDC) at the International Antarctic Center. Instead of carrying all of our gear to the hotel and back every day, we leave most of our cold weather clothing at the CDC and just take a small bag to the hotels. As beautiful as Christchurch is, we hope we'll only be here for 2 days before we're on our way to the ice!
